The Team Goals

To nurture innovation, skills and overall superior expertise good for the development market through engaging in many activities like building market level projects. But we are aware that tech is now diverse and we also have to adapt by building several skills.

Frontend development and Modern CSS and UI design

Frontend development is a critical part of web development, it is the part that users interact with the most and everyone likes something unique something that strikes the eye. Their are many languages used in building UIs and knowing how to use them to come up with website that look as good as the design is necessary

Backend Development and data manipulation.

Their is no question that a website with a good UI also needs a good backend, this is like the engine of the websites. One needs to know how to manipulate data and secure their sites.

API Integration and Use

Knowing how to integrate APIs in your Websites or Apps is also an integral skill that saves people time and also boosts efficiency. APIs are many and now virtually every website comes with one, its up to you to know how to use them and integrate them with your project.

Deployment And Web Management

If you want people to be able to access you website you need to deploy it to an online server and a database. Once deployed you still need to maintain it and keep it up to date, and also fix the bugs whenever they occur.
